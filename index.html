About me

Hey, I am Minh, a 42 years old barber and hairdresser.

My story goes back to 1996 when I started hairdressing. I was 18 years old at the time. It was my first job. Until 2000, I sharpened my skills passionately before taking a completely different direction and working for nearly 16 years in air transport for a large American group.

After an atypical journey and a short break of a few years in my native country, Vietnam, Saigon more precisely. I made an almost unexpected comeback in September 2016 in hairdressing. A return to my first passion and to my beginnings (in music, we call it a " return to the roots "). In short, I am getting back into the profession with determination and I am taking over the management of one of the largest barbershops in Paris in the Montorgueil district on rue Saint-Denis, “ Bonhomme '' until 2020.

In the meantime, I take part in several events (Mondial de la coiffure in Paris, Barber international Trophy in Montpellier, TV report for France 2, etc…). I forge links with several clients, partners and develop my network. But after three years of good and loyal service in this salon, I needed a change of scenery and to stand on my own feet.

Since 2021, I have been an independent and itinerant barber. I have always kept the same philosophy: each client is a unique piece. Hairdressing is an art. And when it is well done, it serves two things: to embellish the person and give it confidence. To achieve this, rigor, memory, and precision are weapons. Each cut is a performance. I always do the maximum.

Minh
